Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Delight Developer App free to use?

Yes! You can use most core features for free. We're excited to launch premium plans with advanced functionalities soon, offering even more powerful tools for your development needs.

Does it support popular frameworks like Flutter or React Native?

Absolutely. Delight Developer is built to support a wide range of mobile development frameworks, including Flutter, React Native, and native apps, allowing for easy preview and comprehensive management directly from your device.

Where is my project data stored and how secure is it?

All your data is securely encrypted and stored on robust Firebase infrastructure, ensuring the highest standards of data privacy and security. Your projects are safe with us.

What kind of devices are supported by the app?

Delight Developer is optimized for a wide range of Android devices, ensuring a smooth experience across various smartphones and tablets. We are continuously working to expand support and ensure compatibility with the latest hardware.
